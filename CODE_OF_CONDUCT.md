# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful, constructive, and professional. We welcome contributors of
all backgrounds and experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing others' private information

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to **conduct@pearlresearch.ai**. Maintainers may
remove comments, close issues, or ban contributors at their discretion.
